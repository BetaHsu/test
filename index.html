Work Name

Creator: Beta HSU Yun Chu

Year: 2021

Medium: Twine + RiTa

Game duration: 5 to 10 minutes

Keywords: #Twine, #Short, #Sci-fi, #Logical, #Lessons, #Lesson

You are a clueless human being, waiting to be educated.
As the main character walks you through the weird lessons/journey, you will find out that some questions are being answered, but some more are raised or remained unsolved…
Link:
Features of the game:
  • Conversation based story
  • Made by Twine, a non-linear interactive storytelling tool.
  • Your decisions matter.
    • Creator’s Note:
    This game was created in April 2021, based on my feelings towards the absurd human nature trying to make sense of everything.
    The writing is generated by hypertext via Twine2 and context-free grammar via RiTa,
    which allows the game to generate different texts according to grammars defined.
    Therefore, different outcomes might appear as the game is being run again and again.
