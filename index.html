Human After All

Creator: BBeta HSU Yun Chu
  Year: 2021
  Medium: Twine + RiTa
  Game duration: 5 to 10 minutes
  Keywords: #Twine, #Short, #Sci-fi, #Mind, #Lessons, #Lesson
You are a clueless human being, waiting to be educated.
As the main character walks you through this weird journey, you pick up some superpowers, have some unspoken questions been solved, but some more spoken questions to be unsolved…
Features of the game:
  • Conversation based story
  • Non-linear interactive storytelling
  • Your decisions matter
    • Creator’s Note:

    This game was created in April 2021, based on my feelings towards the absurd human nature trying to make sense of everything.

    The writing is generated by hypertext via Twine2 and context-free grammar via RiTa, which allows the game to generate different texts according to grammars defined.
    Therefore, different outcomes might appear as the game is being run again and again.

    Artist's bio:

    Beta HSU is currently a year 2 student studying New Media Art in City University of Hong Kong. She is interested in exploring different mediums for artistic expressions.
